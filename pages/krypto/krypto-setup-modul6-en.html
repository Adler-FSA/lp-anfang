Step 6

Warning signals ‚Äì when you should act

Examples where you should take action:

  • You have accounts or devices with large funds that you rarely use or monitor.
  • New legal or tax rules in your country affect what must be reported or how certain platforms are treated.
  • A platform holding meaningful amounts shows frequent outages, poor communication or legal trouble.
  • You notice:
    • you no longer know exactly where which amounts are,
    • your notes are scattered across multiple devices and apps.

Suggestion: ‚ÄúIf at least two of these signals are true for you, schedule a 60‚Äì90 minute cleanup within the next 7 days.‚Äù
