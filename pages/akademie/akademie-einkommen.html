Tippgeber-Modell Referral model

Die zwei Stufen – 20 % und 40 %

Trustyfy arbeitet mit einer klaren Struktur: 20 % Tippgeber-Belohnung für alle, die den kostenlosen Zugang nutzen, und 40 % für alle, die selbst ein Plus- oder Firmenpaket haben.

Wichtig: Es handelt sich nicht um einen Strukturvertrieb mit vielen Ebenen. Du erhältst eine Tippgeber-Belohnung für deine direkten Empfehlungen – jedes Jahr erneut, solange die Pakete aktiv bleiben.

Die Akademie hilft dir, diese Struktur zu verstehen und realistisch einzuordnen – damit du sie klar erklären kannst, ohne etwas zu versprechen, was du nicht halten kannst.

The two levels – 20 % and 40 %

Trustyfy uses a clear structure: 20 % referral reward for everyone using the free access and 40 % for those who have their own Plus or corporate plan.

Important: this is not a multi-level scheme with many layers. You receive a referral reward for your own direct recommendations – every year again, as long as the plans stay active.

The Academy helps you understand this structure and put it into context, so you can explain it clearly without promising anything you cannot keep.

From side income to yearly amount

To get a feeling for what is possible, a rough frame helps. For simplicity we assume 1 USDT ≈ 1 €.

Example 1 – approx. €2,000/year:

At the 40 % referral level, one active Plus plan creates about $24 per year, a Corporate Pro plan around $240 and a Crypto Companies plan about $1,200. A mix of roughly 60 Plus users and a few corporate clients gets you close to €2,000 per year.

Example 2 – approx. €24,000/year:

If you keep going consistently and your network grows, e.g. 200 Plus users, 30 Corporate Pro companies and some Crypto Companies clients can move you into the range of an additional yearly income.

This is not a guarantee or promise – but a realistic possibility for people who are willing to keep going, have regular conversations and use the Academy as a co-pilot in everyday life.
