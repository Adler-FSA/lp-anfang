  • keine naive oder hektische Nutzerführung

  • no naive or hectic user guidance
  • not a reading-only course, but a work and protection course

  • clear structure despite large scope
  • recognizable page types throughout
  • tool logic with real practical value
  • build in evidence, cost, and conflict logic
  • integrate official signposts into relevant pages
