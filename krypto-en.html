üìå Short

Both a crypto address and an IBAN are unique payment destinations. IBANs are issued by banks and standardized; crypto addresses are generated decentral¬≠ly by algorithms. Result: more freedom and security ‚Äî and more self-responsibility.

  • Security: Crypto uses cryptography ‚Üí higher security baseline.
  • Privacy: Crypto can be pseudonymous; IBAN is always personally tied.

Cons crypto: complexity, user-error risks, legal gray areas.

Pros IBAN: standardized, familiar, globally recognized.

Cons IBAN: bank-dependent, fees, can be frozen or seized.
